## Package Standard

Each instructor pack should include:

- quick-check answers
- worked paths for applied problems
- one strong and one weak chapter artifact with annotations
- a short rubric for the synthesis artifact
- common misconception notes

Keep public materials focused on hints, templates, and high-level rubrics. Keep complete artifact solutions and scoring notes private.
